Engine Cables

The cables need to be in the correct order. This order needs to be used in the health check (only the first letter of the color).
Example: correct order 'red blue green' => health check to '/rbg'

The correct order is defined as follows:

  • There are 2 red cables, 2 green cable, 1 blue cable and 1 yellow cable.
  • Red cables must not touch green cables or other red cables.
  • One of the green cables must lie left of the blue cable.
  • The last cable must be a red cable.
  • The green cables must stay together.
